Welcome to Pig Dice: The Game

Here is how the Game Works:

There are 2 players, Player 1 and Player 2, The goal is to reach 100 (or more) points using ONE six sided dice and the built in Score Keeper.

On a turn, a player rolls the die repeatedly to accumulate as many points as possible, However should the player roll a 1, their accumulated points for that round are lost, and thus it goes to the next player.

A Player can also choose to hold (Stop rolling the die) if they don't want to take the change of rolling a 1 thus ending their turn and getting their accumulated points for that turn added to their total score.

Once a Player reaches 100 or more, that Player wins.
